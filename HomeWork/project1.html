Biography

Abhishek Tyagi was born in Nayagaon village of morena district in Madhay Pradesh state in India on 15 July 2022. When he was a child of three year old, his family send him with his aunty to gwalior to study. From nursery to 2nd class he studied in the novel foundation school in balvant bagar gwalior, then due to incedent he has to study 2nd class in his town in morena. After that he continued his study in Ramakrishna Vidya Mandir C.B.S.E currently know as RKM (Ramakrishna Mission) from class 3rd to class 12th. Current he is in his 3rd year of Bachelor's degree from Samrat Ashok Technological Institute in Vidisha (A government aided institute).
